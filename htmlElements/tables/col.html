Col - <col>

The col tag specifies the properties for each column within a colgroup element.

It is used to apply styles to entire columns instead of repeating the styles for each cell, for each row.


Examples:

heading 1 heading 2 heading 3 heading 4
cell 1 cell 2 cell 3 cell 4
cell 1 cell 2 cell 3 cell 4
