Address - <address>

This element definesthe contact information for the author/owner of a document or an article.

The contact informationj can be an email address, URL, physical address, phone mumber, social media handle etc.

The text in the address element usually renders in italic and browsers will always add a line break before and after the address element.


Examples:

Written by Stephen Mogg
Email us atrandomEmail@.com
