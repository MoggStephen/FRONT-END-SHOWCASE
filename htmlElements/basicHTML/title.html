Title - <title>

This element defines the title of the document. The title must be text only and is shown in the browsers title bar or in the page's tab.

The title tag is required in all documents.

The contents of the title are important for search engine optimization so they can decide the listing of the pages in search results.

The title element:

  • Defines the title in the browser toolbar
  • Provides the title for trhe page when it is added to favourites
  • Displays a title for the page in search enigne results.

    • Tips for creating good titles:

  • Go for longer descriptive title(avoid one or two word titles)
  • Search engines will display about 50-60 characters of the title so try not to have titles longer than that
  • Do not use just a list of words as the title.(This may reduce the pages position in search results)

    • Make the title as accurate and as meaningingfull as possible.
