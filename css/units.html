vmin

Due to the nature of the viewports. Different viewports will have different sizes.

On a typical computer screen the width is larger than the height therefore vmin would choose the vh value as this is the smaller value.

Whereas on a mobile phone the vmin would choose the vw value as the width of the screen is smaller.

vmax

Due to the nature of the viewports. Different viewports will have different sizes.

On a typical computer screen the width is larger than the height therefore vmax would choose the w value as this is the larger value.

Whereas on a mobile phone the vmax would choose the vh value as the height of the screen is larger.
